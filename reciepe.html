Jerk Chicken Reciepe

"width:500px"

Description

No time to make it to the Caribbean? We've got you covered. Jerk Chicken is a popular Jamaican Dish, that's marinated in natural spices.

Ingredients

For the marinade:

  • 1/4 c. lime juice
  • 1/3 c. low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 piece fresh ginger, peeled and minced
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp. ground allspice
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • Freshly ground black pepper

For the Chicken:

  • 1 1/2 lb. bone-in chicken drumsticks and thighs
  • Lime slices, for serving
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Directions

  1. Make marinade: In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and whisk until combined.
  2. Add chicken and toss to coat. Transfer to the refrigerator and marinate at least 30 minutes and up to overnight.
  3. Heat grill to high. Grill chicken until charred and cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Serve with lime slices and green onions.
